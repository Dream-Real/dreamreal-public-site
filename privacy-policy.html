Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

DreamReal ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how DreamReal handles user data when using the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

DreamReal only collects the information that you authorize through the Facebook Login process. Depending on the permissions you grant, we may access:

  • Your public Facebook profile (name, profile picture)
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Your posts, photos or content that you explicitly allow the app to access
  • Your Facebook user ID

We do not collect any data outside of what is provided through the Facebook API and authorized by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

DreamReal uses your information only for the purpose of displaying your Facebook content inside the app. Specifically:

  • To show your posts, photos, and profile information
  • To personalize your visual feed
  • To allow optional features such as mood tagging (which is stored locally on your device)

We do not sell, share, or transfer your information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

DreamReal does not store your Facebook data on external servers. All retrieved content is processed locally on your device and cached temporarily in the app (e.g., photos or posts) to improve performance.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed.

4. Access Tokens

Facebook access tokens are stored securely on your device using industry-standard mobile secure storage practices. They are never transmitted to any external server.

5. Third-Party Services

DreamReal relies on the Facebook Graph API to retrieve your authorized content. All interactions with Facebook comply with their Platform Terms and Data Protection policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

DreamReal is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Your Rights

Since DreamReal does not store your data on external servers, all data management actions can be performed directly on your device:

  • Revoke Facebook permissions from your Facebook Settings
  • Clear app cache
  • Uninstall the app to erase all local data
