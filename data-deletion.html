Data Deletion Instructions

DreamReal Social Mirror does not store any personal data on its own servers. All Facebook data displayed in the app (profile picture, posts, email, link, etc.) is retrieved directly from the Facebook Graph API and remains stored on Facebook’s servers.

How to delete your Facebook data

If you want your data to be deleted from DreamReal Social Mirror, you can simply remove our app from your Facebook account settings.

To do so:

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click on Settings
  3. Select Apps and Websites
  4. Find DreamReal Social Mirror
  5. Click Remove

Once removed, DreamReal Social Mirror will no longer have access to any of your Facebook data.
